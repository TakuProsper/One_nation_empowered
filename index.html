    About

    About Us

    • In the wake of various global dynamics, Zimbabwe has emerged as a diverse mosaic of ethnicities, languages, and cultures. The legacy of colonialism has left a complex tapestry of oppression, resistance, and cultural transformation. One Nation Empowered was formed to address the challenges and embrace the opportunities presented by this diversity.
    • One Nation Empowered is dedicated to fostering unity and empowering youth in a nation rich with diverse ethnicities, languages, and cultures. Born from the legacy of colonialism, Zimbabwe faces the challenge of uniting its varied peoples. Our organization addresses this by promoting dialogue, inclusive policies, economic equity, and cultural appreciation.

    • We strive to awaken the conscience of the Zimbabwean masses through grassroots efforts, denouncing disunity and marginalization. One Nation Empowered nurtures a new generation of patriotic, determined, and disciplined youth leaders who uphold the principles of unity and oneness. Together, we aim to build a harmonious and resilient society, preserving Zimbabwe's cultural heritage and collective identity.
    • Join us in our mission to create a unified and empowered Zimbabwe.

    Our Vision and Mission

    We envision a Zimbabwe where every individual has the opportunity to thrive, where no one is left behind, and where our collective potential is harnessed for the greater good.

    • 01 Education and Skills Development

      At the heart of our mission lies the empowerment of communities through education and skills development. We firmly believe that education forms the bedrock of societal advancement. Our commitment is to advocate for and actively participate in delivering high-quality education and vocational training, empowering individuals to forge a path towards a brighter future for themselves and their loved ones. By promoting accessible education and fostering skill development, we aim to foster community resilience and sustainable prosperity for all.

    • 02 Health and Well-being

      We are dedicated to increasing access to comprehensive healthcare, promoting preventive measures, and fostering a culture of wellness. By achieving this, we aim to build strong, resilient communities that contribute to the overall prosperity and growth of our nation.

    • 03 Economic Empowerment

      We aim to empower communities through economic empowerment, focusing on championing entrepreneurship, fostering job creation, and promoting sustainable economic growth. By supporting local businesses and encouraging innovation, we aim to uplift and strengthen our communities. Through these efforts, we strive to create opportunities that enable individuals and organizations to thrive, contributing to a prosperous and resilient society for all.

    • 03 Social Inclusion

      We aim to empower communities through social inclusion, embracing the richness of diversity and ensuring no one is marginalized or left behind. We are committed to creating an inclusive environment where every individual is valued and respected. By promoting equality of opportunity and fostering a sense of belonging, we aim to build strong and cohesive communities. Through collaboration and mutual support, we strive to create a society where everyone can contribute fully to its social fabric and collective prosperity.

    Philosophy & Core Values

    Our Philosophy and Core Values

    Patriotism

    We strive to inspire passion amongst our youths to serve our country with dedication and honor, embracing its ideals and striving for its prosperity.

    Collaboration

    Through collaboration, we aim to build bridges that connect people, ideas, and resources, empowering collective impact and success.

    Discipline

    We aim to instill discipline in our minds and actions, cultivating self-control, perseverance, and responsibility to achieve excellence and shape a brighter future.

    Excellence

    Through a relentless pursuit of excellence, we seek to transform lives, revolutionize industries, and create a better world, where greatness is the norm and excellence is the hallmark.

    Hard-work

    We strive to instill a strong work ethic in our youths, empowering them to embrace the value of hard work, perseverance, and determination, to achieve their goals and build a brighter future.

    Obedience

    With obedience as our guiding principle, we aim to cultivate a culture of discipline, compliance, and responsibility, ensuring that our actions align with our values and our nation's ideals.

    Commitment

    We believe that commitment is the backbone of progress, and therefore, we commit to standing by our principles, supporting one another, and working together to build a brighter future for our country.

    Programmes

    Our Programmes

    Unity

    We will carry out programmes that will help to instil a sense of pride and love for our country among the citizens. Lectures, presentations and seminars will be held that are aimed at fostering a deep understanding of our heritage, history, and cultural diversity.

    Active Citizenship

    We encourage and empower the Zimbabweans, particularly the youth to actively participate in the democratic process, promoting civic engagement, and responsible leadership. Through education, awareness campaigns, and community initiatives, we strive to create a generation of responsible citizens who contribute positively to society.

    Empowerment

    We are dedicated to empowering minds by providing educational opportunities, skills development programs, and mentoring. We believe in nurturing the potential of every individual, fostering their personal growth, and equipping them with the tools to succeed.

    Social Justice

    We will advocate for social justice, equality, and inclusivity, standing against all forms of discrimination, prejudice, and injustice. We endeavour to create a society where every individual has equal rights and opportunities, regardless of their background or circumstances.

    Environmental Stewardship

    Recognizing the importance of environmental sustainability, we are committed to protecting and preserving our natural resources for future generations. Through environmental initiatives, awareness campaigns, and sustainable practices, we strive to be responsible stewards of the environment.

    F.A.Q

    Frequently Asked Questions

    What are the main objectives of One Nation Empowered?

    To unite all Zimbabweans in their diversity into a vibrant economic, social and political force capable of formulating concrete means and methods of overcoming disunity and creating oneness in Zimbabwe

    To build a common Zimbabwean identity that emerges from cultural synthesis, dreams of socio-economic progress, sovereignty, and renaissance that help to collectively work towards a brighter and shared future characterized by the celebration of diversity within unity.

    To conduct and coordinate landmark youth events with the ability of installing a sense of national pride and national identity in order to awaken a culture of responsibility within the citizenry

    To engage with various stakeholders and organisations, as well as engage in any other activities that may be expedient and necessary to further the objectives and goals ofthe organisation.
